# OwlGo – Optimization Summary

This report summarizes the results of a campus access optimization model designed to reduce walking time between parking, classrooms, and departmental buildings.

## Key Outcomes
- Reduced parking-to-class travel time from 10–12 minutes to under 5 minutes
- Reduced travel time between major-related classes to under 2 minutes
- Identified pedestrian bottlenecks and low-cost infrastructure improvements

## Tools Used
- Python
- Google OR-Tools
- Excel-based data modeling
